📖 Platform Documentation

🏢 Creating a Restaurant
Go to Restaurants → Click "+ Create Restaurant" → Fill in name, address, plan, and admin credentials. The restaurant gets its own admin login and QR codes automatically.
💳 Managing Subscriptions
Go to Subscriptions → See all trial, active, and expiring restaurants. Click "View" to update their plan or expiry date.
📢 Sending Broadcasts
Use the red "Broadcast" button in the header for global alerts. Use the "Notify" button in Restaurants for targeted messages.
🚫 Suspending a Restaurant
Go to Restaurants → Click "Suspend". This immediately blocks their admin login. Click "Restore" to reactivate.
